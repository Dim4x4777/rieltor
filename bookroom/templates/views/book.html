Harry Potter and the Cursed Child
J. K. Rouling

Harry Potter and the Cursed Child Parts One and Two is the official script book for the play of the same name written by J. K. Rowling, John Tiffany, and Jack Thorne. It is the eighth story published in the Harry Potter book series. J. K. Rowling wanted the plot of the play to remain a secret for as long as possible. Following the opening night of the first preview, she started a #KeepTheSecrets campaign. Following the opening night of the first preview, she started a campaign campaign.

Nazariy Mandebura
Jack Thorne’s play, based on a new story by himself, Rowling and John Tiffany, strikes a clever balance between recognisable Pottery and a new departure. It begins where the final (seventh) book left off, with the middle-aged Harry watching his son board the Hogwarts Express. Its emotional centre belongs partly to the younger wizard generation, but not entirely.
